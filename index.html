2015-07-14

We are excited to announce preparations for the first Nix conference — NixCon 2015.

NixCon 2015 will be held in Berlin as a 2-day (weekend) event in November, with talks and unsessions. Sprinting space will be also provided for days before and after the conference.

The program isn’t definite yet, so we’d like to ask you to help us form it by submitting ideas and wishes: http://nixcon.useresponse.com. Based on the feedback, we’ll do our best to organize a nice event which would make our community even stronger.

At the end of July we will release a Call for Presentations including dates and additional details. Once we know more, we will update the conference website with news: http://conf.nixos.org. You can also follow us on twitter (@NixConBerlin) for more announcements.

If your organization is interested in sponsoring the conference in any way, NixCon is a way to make smart developers aware of your company. NixCon 2015 is organized by volunteers and leftover funds (if any) will go to the Nix foundation. We are also looking for more volunteers, especially from the Berlin area, to help us organize an amazing conference.

NixCon team, conf@nixos.org
